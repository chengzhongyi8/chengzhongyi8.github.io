About

I received my Ph.D. in Soil Science from Zhejiang University in June 2025, under the supervision of Prof. Jiachun Shi, Prof. Yan He, and Prof. Laosheng Wu.

In August 2025, I was selected for the JAAS Zijin Talent Program for Young Leading Talents and joined the Institute of Agricultural Resources and Environment, Jiangsu Academy of Agricultural Sciences, as an Assistant Researcher. I am currently a member of the Soil Pollution Remediation Innovation Team, led by Prof. Yan Gao.

My research focuses on microbiome-mediated mitigation of cadmium accumulation in crops grown on contaminated farmland. I study how root-associated core microbiota influence cadmium bioavailability, rhizosphere immobilization, nutrient acquisition, and crop adaptation. By combining soil analysis, multi-omics, microbial isolation, genome-scale metabolic modeling, and synthetic communities, my work aims to develop microbiome-based strategies for low-cadmium crop production and soil health restoration.
